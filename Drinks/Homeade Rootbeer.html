Homemade Root Beer

1-gallon water- cold
3 cups sugar
3 TBS root beer extract 1 lb. dry ice

Add sugar and root beer extract to cold water and mix thoroughly in a large open container until the sugar has dissolved. Do not cover the container with a lid while preparing root beer since pressure created may cause container to explode.

Add dry ice in a well-ventilated area, (do not cover) and stir frequently to prevent the dry ice from clumping to the bottom of the container. Continue to stir until all the dry ice has dissolved.
