Dill Pretzels

1 bottle of popcorn oil 2 tbs dill
1⁄4 tsp garlic powder
1⁄4 tsp onion powder
1 pkg. pretzels

Add all the spices to the bottle of popcorn oil. Shake well. In a bowl, mix the package of pretzels and 1⁄2 of the bottle of oil and spices. Let soak overnight, stir often.
