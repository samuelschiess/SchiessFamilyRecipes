Fruit Salsa with Cinnamon Chips


Back to Dips and Appetizers

Fruit Salsa with cinnamon chips- Katie Hudson

Salsa:

2 granny smith apples- peeled, cored, and diced
1 pkg. strawberries
2 kiwis- peeled and sliced
1 small orange

Chop apples, strawberries, and kiwi. Place the fruit in a bowl. Roll the orange on the counter to release juice inside. Cut in half and squeeze juice over salsa. Stir together.

Preheat oven to 450*. Mix sugar and cinnamon. Slightly moisten tortillas using your fingertips dipped in water. Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar. Cut into wedges using a pizza cutter. Place on a cookie sheet sprayed with cooking spray. Bake for 5-7 minutes.

Other possible chips: Apple cinnamon rice cakes or cinnamon pita chips
