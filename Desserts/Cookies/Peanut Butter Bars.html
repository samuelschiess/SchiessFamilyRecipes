Peanut Butter Bars

Bars:

2/3 cup peanut butter
1 cup butter
1 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp soda
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups flour
2 cups oatmeal

Mix the first 5 ingredients. Add soda and vanilla. Mix. Then add oatmeal and flour. Mix well. Spread on a large greased cookie sheet with 1” space around the edges. Bake at 350* for 18-20 minutes (do not over bake!) Cool completely.

Chocolate Frosting:

6 TBS butter
3 TBS cocoa
½ tsp vanilla
4 TBS milk
3 cups powdered sugar

Mix all ingredients together until smooth. Frost the bars.
