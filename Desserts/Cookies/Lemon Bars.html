Lemon Bars- Ann Eyre

Crust:

2 cups flour
1 cup butter- softened
¼ tsp salt
½ cup powdered sugar

Filling:

2 cups sugar
6 TBS lemon juice
¼ cup flour
4 eggs- beaten

Mix ingredients for crust. Press crust in 9x13” pan and bake at 350* for 15 minutes. Cool 5-10 minutes. Mix the ingredients for the filling together. Pour on the top of the crust and bake for 25 more minutes. Sprinkle with powdered sugar while hot.
