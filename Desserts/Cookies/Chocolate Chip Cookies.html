Chocolate Chip Cookies

1 cup butter (2 sticks)
¾ cup sugar
¾ cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla
2 ½ cup flour
Chocolate chips

In a bowl mix together butter, sugar, and eggs until creamed. Add soda, salt, and vanilla. Mix in flour and lastly chocolate chips. Drop by spoonful onto a greased cookies sheet. Bake at 350* for 10-12 minutes.
