Homemade Oreo Cookies

Cookies:

1 Devil’s food cake mix
2 eggs
¾ cup shortening

Mix all the ingredients together. Roll into balls and place on and ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 350* for 8-10 minutes. (Do not over bake!) Cool completely.

Filling:

½ cup butter- softened
8 oz. cream cheese- softened
2 cups powdered sugar
½ tsp vanilla

In a bowl, beat together all the ingredients until smooth. Spread the filling onto the bottom of a cookie and place
