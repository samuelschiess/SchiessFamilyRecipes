Chocolate Waffle Cookies

½ cup butter
¼ cup shortening
2 ½ tsp cocoa
4 eggs- beaten
1 ¼ cups sugar
½ tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups flour

Mix shortening, butter, sugar, cocoa, and eggs together, then mix in, salt and vanilla. Lastly mix in flour. Drop on waffle iron, 1 teaspoonful at a time. Cook 40-60 seconds. Frost with chocolate butter cream frosting.
