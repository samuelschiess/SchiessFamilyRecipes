Lana’s Sugar Cookies

¾ cup sugar
½ cup shortening
½ cup milk
2 eggs
1 ½ TBS baking powder
½ tsp salt
½ tsp baking soda
½ TBS vanilla
3 cups flour

In a bowl mix sugar, shortening, milk, and eggs. Add baking powder, salt, soda, and vanilla and mix. Add flour and mix well. Roll out dough on floured surface and cut with cookie cutters. Place on greased cookie sheet. Bake at 350* for 8-10 minutes. (or less).
