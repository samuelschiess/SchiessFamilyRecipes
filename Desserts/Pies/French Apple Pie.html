French Apple Pie

Filling:

6-7 cups apples- peeled and sliced (Golden Delicious makes a softer filling)
¾ cups sugar
Cinnamon- a dash
Nutmeg- a dash
1 uncooked pie crust

Mix all the ingredients together. Place a pie crust in a 9” pie plate, put in the filling.

Topping:

½ cup butter
½ cup brown sugar
1 cup flour

Mix ingredients. Sprinkle over the top of the pie. Bake at 350* for 50-60 minutes.
