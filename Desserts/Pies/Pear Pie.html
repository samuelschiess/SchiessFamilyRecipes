Pear Pie (Book Group Recipe)

9” pie crust- uncooked
2 cans pears- 15 oz. (or 6-7 fresh pears- peeled and sliced)
1 cup sugar
¼ cup flour
1 cup heavy cream
1 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp lemon juice

Mix all the ingredients together. Pour into the pie crust. Bake at 400* for 30-40 minutes.
