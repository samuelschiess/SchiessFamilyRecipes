Fresh Peach Pie- Hub Café in Heber

2- 9” pie crusts- baked (or graham cracker crusts)
6 cups fresh peaches- washed and sliced

Glaze:

1 cup puree peaches
1 cup sugar
3 TBS cornstarch
½ cup water

Puree 1 cup of peaches in a blender. Then, in a pot, mix sugar and cornstarch together. Gradually stir in water and pureed peaches. Cook over medium heat until boiling, stir constantly. Cool completely. Mix in the remaining peaches.

Filling:

4 oz. cream cheese
1 ½ TBS butter
½ tsp vanilla
1 cup powdered sugar

In a bowl, beat cream cheese, butter, vanilla, and powdered sugar until smooth. Cover the bottom of the pie crust with cream cheese mixture. Then pour the glaze on top. Serve with fresh whipped cream.
