Pumpkin Pie

2 eggs
1 ½ cup pumpkin- canned, or if using fresh pumpkin- baked and mashed
¾ cup sugar
¼ tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon
½ tsp ginger
¼ tsp cloves
1 can evaporated milk
1 uncooked pies crust

Blend ingredients together in a blender until smooth. Put the crust in a 9” pie pan, pour in the filling. Bake at 350* for 60 minutes.
