Fresh Apple Cake- Joyce Shumway

In a large bowl, mix:

4 cups Golden Delicious apples- peeled and sliced
2 cups sugar

In a small bowl, mix:

2 eggs
¾ cups oil
2 tsp vanilla

Add this to the apple mixture.

Add to the above mixture:

2 cups flour
1 tsp soda
½ tsp salt
2 tsp cinnamon
1 cup walnuts or pecans- optional

Pour batter into greased 9x13” cakepan. Bake at 325* for 40-50 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean when inserted to the middle of the cake.

Carmel sauce

In a saucepan, combine:

1 cup sugar
½ cup evaporated milk
½ cup butter
1 tsp vanilla

Bring to a boil. Boil for 3 minutes or until thickened. Serve on top of a slice of cake.
