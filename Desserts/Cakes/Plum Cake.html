Plum Cake- Joyce Shumway (Excellent for fancy occasions)

1 ½ cups sugar
2 cups plums- pureed
1 ¼ tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp cloves
½ cup oil
3 eggs
2 cups flour
Nuts- optional

Mix all the ingredients together. Pour batter into a 9x13” pan. (For a fancier cake bake in 2- 9” round pans). Bake at 350* for 40 minutes. Sprinkle top with powdered sugar or frost with your favorite cream cheese frosting.
