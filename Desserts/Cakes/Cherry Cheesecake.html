Cherry Cheesecake- Ann Eyre (The Soft Kind)

Crust:

1 ½ pkgs graham crackers- crushed to powder with a rolling pin
1 cube butter-melted
¼ cup powdered sugar

Topping:

1 carton of cool whip (regular size)
1 pkg cream cheese-8 oz.
½ cup powdered sugar
1 can cherry pie filling

Mix crushed graham crackers, melted butter and powdered sugar. Press into a 9x13” cakepan. In another bowl whip together whipped topping, cream cheese, and powdered sugar with a mixer until smooth. Spread on the crust. Spread cherries on top of the cream layer. Refrigerate until served.
