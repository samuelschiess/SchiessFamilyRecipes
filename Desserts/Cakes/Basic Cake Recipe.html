Basic Cake Recipe- Caroline’s Favorite

2 ¼ cup flour
1 ½ cup sugar
3 ½ tsp baking powder
½ tsp salt
½ cup butter- cubed
4 egg whites
¾ cup milk- divided
1 tsp vanilla
½ tsp almond extract
(½ cup cocoa powder- if you want a chocolate cake)

In a bowl, whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, (cocoa powder), and salt. Beat in butter until crumbly. Add egg whites, one at a time, beating well after each one. Gradually beat in ¼ cup milk and extracts, beat on medium until light and fluffy, about 2 minutes. Gradually beat in remaining milk. Grease and flour 2 round cake pans. Pour in the batter. Bake at 350* for 25-30 minutes (or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean). Cool in pans for 10 minutes before removing to wire racks to cool. Cool completely before frosting.
