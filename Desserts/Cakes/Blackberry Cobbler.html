Blackberry Cobbler- Joyce Shumway

1 cup flour
1 cup sugar
¼ tsp salt
2 tsp baking powder
¾ cup milk
½ cup butter- melted
2 cups blackberries- fresh or frozen
2 tsp almond extract

Mix flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder in an 8x8” glass baking dish. Stir in milk, blend. Pour melted butter over dough. Toss blackberries with almond extract. Spoon over top of dough. Bake at 350* for 40-50 minutes or until dough rises to top and is brown. Serve warm with vanilla ice cream.
