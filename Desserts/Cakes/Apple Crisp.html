Apple Crisp

1 cup flour
¾ cup oatmeal- rolled oats
1 cup brown sugar-packed
1 tsp cinnamon
½ cup butter- softened
4 cups apples- peeled and chopped
1 cup sugar
2 TBS cornstarch
1 cup water
1 tsp vanilla

Combine the first 4 ingredients. Cut in butter until crumbly. Press half into a greased 2-quart baking pan or 9” baking pan. Cover with apples. In a saucepan, combine sugar, cornstarch, water, and vanilla. Cook and stir until thick and clear. Pour over apples. Sprinkle with remaining crumb mixture. Bake at 350* for 1 hour.

If using canned apple pie filling, follow the recipe, but replace apples and cooked sauce with canned filling.
