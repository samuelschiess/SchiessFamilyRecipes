Texas Sheet Cake- Lion House Recipes

½ cup butter- softened
½ cup shortening
2 TBS cocoa powder
1 cup water
2 cups flour
1 ½ cup sugar
½ cup buttermilk
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp vanilla
2 eggs- beaten
¼ tsp salt

In a pot, bring the butter, shortening, cocoa, and water to a boil. In a separate bowl, shift together flour and sugar. Pour butter mixture into the bowl. Mix well. Mix in buttermilk, soda, vanilla, eggs, and salt. Blend well. Pour into a large, greased cookie sheet. Bake at 400* for 20 minutes.

Frosting for Sheet Cake:

½ cup butter
2 TBS cocoa powder
1 tsp vanilla
6 TBS milk
4 cups powdered sugar
1 cup nuts- chopped (optional)

In a pot, melt butter and add cocoa, vanilla, and milk. Bring to a boil. Remove from the heat and add powdered sugar and nuts. Mix well. Frost cake while it is hot.
