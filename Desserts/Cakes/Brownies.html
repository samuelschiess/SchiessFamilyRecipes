Brownies- Great Grandma- Glenna Tippets

In a pot, melt together and bring to a boil.

½ cup butter
1/3 cup oil
1 cup water
3 TBS cocoa powder

Add:

2 cups flour
2 cups sugar
1 tsp baking soda
2 eggs
½ cup milk (or buttermilk)
1 tsp vanilla

Mix all the ingredients together and pour into a greased sheet cake pan. Bake at 375* for 15-17 minutes.

Frosting to go with the brownies:

½ cup butter- softened
5 TBS milk
3 ½ TBS cocoa powder
1 pkg powdered sugar (a bit less)
1 cup nuts- chopped and sprinkled on top

In a pot, melt all the ingredients together while stirring. Frost the brownies while it is hot.
