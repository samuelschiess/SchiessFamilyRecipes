Devil’s Food Cake- Great Grandma-Dorotha Thomas

1 ½ cup sugar
2 eggs
½ cup shortening
½ cup cocoa powder
2 tsp soda in ½ cup milk
2 cups flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup boiling water

Combine the soda and milk together. Set aside. Bring water to a boil. Mix all remaining ingredients together, then add boiling water and milk and soda, beat well. Bake at 350* for 25 minutes in a well-greased pan.
