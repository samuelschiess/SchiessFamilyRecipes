Bean Brownies- The Evenson Family (Book Group Dessert)

1 cup cooked or canned black or pinto beans-pureed in blender or food processer
6 TBS butter- melted
4 eggs
2 cups sugar
2/3 cup cocoa powder
2 tsp vanilla
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 cup flour
½ cup pecans or walnuts- optional

In a blender or food processor, puree beans with melted butter. Put into large mixing bowl. Mix in the eggs. Then mix in sugar, cocoa, vanilla, baking soda, and salt. Then add flour and stir until well blended. In a greased 9x13” pan, bake at 350* for 20-30 minutes. Do not overbake!
