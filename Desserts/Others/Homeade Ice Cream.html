Homemade Ice Cream

2 cups milk
1 TBS flour
¾ cup sugar
2 egg yolks- slightly beaten
1-pint heavy cream
1 TBS vanilla
¼ tsp salt

Heat 1 ½ cup of milk until it’s very hot. Mix flour, sugar, and ½ cup of milk in a bowl. Add hot milk slowly and stir until smooth. Return to the pan and cook, stirring constantly for about 8 minutes. Pour a little of the milk mixture over the yolks, then stir the yolks into the pan. Cook for another minute, then cool. Add the cream, vanilla, and salt. Freeze in an electric ice cream maker. (For our ice cream maker, put the container in the mixer. Layer 5” of ice around the outside, then add a layer of salt, then ice and repeat until it is above the level of ice cream inside. Let it run for 30 minutes). The ice cream will be soft serve. If you would like it harder, place the container in the freezer for 1-2 hours before serving. (For our family we need to double the recipe.)
