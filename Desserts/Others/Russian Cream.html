Russian Cream (Book Group Dessert)

1 ½ cup cool whip (or whipping cream)
16 oz. sour cream
1 cup sugar
1 pkg unflavored gelatin
½ cup very hot water
1 TBS vanilla
12 oz. fruit- mixed berries, bananas, strawberries, blueberries raspberries, etc.

Dissolve gelatin in hot water and set aside. Cream sugar and whipping cream. Microwave it until sugar is dissolved (2 minutes- don’t boil). Stir in container, whisk in sour cream, gelatin, and vanilla. Refrigerate until cool (3-4 hours). Put into cups and top with berries. Serves 12 (serving size: ½ to ¾ cup)
