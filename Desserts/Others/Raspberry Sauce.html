Raspberry Sauce

2 ½ cups raspberries- divided
¾ cup sugar
½ cup water
½ tsp vanilla
2 tsp cornstarch
2 TBS water
1 TBS butter

Place 1 ½ cups of raspberries in saucepan. Add sugar and water. Heat until boiling and sugar is dissolved- stir often. Reduce heat and add vanilla. In a separate bowl, combine cornstarch and water. Add it in the saucepan and bring to a boil over medium heat. Reduce heat to low and cook for 4 minutes or until thickened. Remove from heat, add butter, and let cool for 15 minutes. Add the remaining raspberries and serve over ice cream, or anything else to make it yummy.
