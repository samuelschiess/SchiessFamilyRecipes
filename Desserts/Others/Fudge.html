Fudge

1 pkg of chocolate chips
1 can sweetened condensed milk
2 TBS butter
¼ tsp salt
1 TBS vanilla
1 cup nuts- optional

In a saucepan or microwave, melt chocolate chips, butter, sweetened condensed milk, and salt together. Stir until smooth. Remove from heat and add vanilla and nuts. Line a 9x13” cakepan with wax paper. Spread fudge evenly in the pan. Chill for 3 hours. Turn fudge onto a cutting board. Peel off paper and cut into squares. Store tightly covered at room temperature or in refrigerator.
