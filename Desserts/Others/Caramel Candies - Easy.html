Caramels- (The candy)- Easy Version

1 cup butter
2 ½ cup brown sugar (White sugar will give you a lighter colored caramel)
1/8 tsp salt
1 cup light corn syrup
1 can condensed milk- 15 oz.
1 TBS vanilla

In a saucepan, cook all the ingredients over medium heat for 6-8 minutes to the soft ball stage (230*). Place in 9x13” greased pan. Cool. Cut into squares.
