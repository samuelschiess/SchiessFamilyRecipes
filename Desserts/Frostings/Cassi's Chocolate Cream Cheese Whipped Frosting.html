Cassi’s Chocolate Cream Cheese Whipped Frosting

3 TBS cocoa powder
1 ½ cups whipping cream- cold
8 oz. cream cheese-cold
1 cup sugar (regular sugar, not powdered)
1/8 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla

Mix cocoa powder with cream until it forms a paste. Chill. Then add cream cheese and the other ingredients. Beat with a mixer until the sugar is no longer gritty. Drizzle in cream and beat until it forms stiff peaks.
