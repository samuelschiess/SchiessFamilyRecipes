Perfect Cupcake Frosting and Filling- Our Best Bites

3 TBS flour
½ cup milk
½ cup butter- 1 stick
½ cup sugar (regular sugar, not powdered)
1 tsp vanilla- or other flavor extracts

Whisk together the flour and milk. Heat in a small saucepan on medium heat. Whisk continuously until it starts to thicken. Let it cook, while stirring with a rubber spatula, until you can start to see the bottom of the pan. Continue to cook until mixture has the consistency of thick pudding or paste. Put mixture in the fridge and let it cool completely. You can also cool it in the freezer to hurry up the process, just don’t let it freeze.

Using the whisk attachment on your mixer, beat the butter and the sugar for 1-2 minutes until well combined and fluffy. While beating add in the thickened milk mixture and the flavor extracts. Beat to combine and then scrape down the sides. Mixture will separate and look messy, keep beating! Continue beating until mixture comes together and is light and fluffy, about 7-8 minutes, but time varies. Take a sample of frosting between your fingers, frosting is done when light and fluffy and sugar granules are dissolved.
