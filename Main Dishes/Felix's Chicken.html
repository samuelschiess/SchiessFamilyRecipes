4-6 chicken breasts
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 pkg. (8 oz.) cream cheese- cut up into chunks
1 pkg. Zesty Italian Mix (an herb marinade found by the taco seasoning packages in the store)

Place all the ingredients into a crockpot and stir. Cook on high for 1-2 hours, and then cook on low 2 hours. Or you can just cook it on low the whole time for 5 hours. Serve over rice.
