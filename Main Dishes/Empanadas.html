Empanadas

1 lb. meat (hamburger, left over roast, or chicken)
1 can olives- sliced
2 pkgs. Pillsbury Grand rolls (8 in each pkg.)
3-4 eggs- hard boiled
Â½ cup raisins

Roll out rolls on floured surface with rolling pin. Place meat in the middle of each roll. Add some sliced olives, eggs, and raisins. Fold roll in half so it looks like a half moon. Seal the open edge by squishing it with a fork. Place on an ungreased cookie sheet and bake at 375* for 13 minutes.
