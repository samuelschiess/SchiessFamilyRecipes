BTC Toasted Subs- Heather Dorius

Hoagie Buns
1-pound cooked bacon
3 tomatoes
Ranch Dressing
Salt and pepper to taste
Slices of Mozzarella, Colby Jack, and Cheddar cheese
Sprinkling of Parmesan cheese

Bake the bacon in the oven on a large cookie sheet at 400* for 15 minutes or until crispy brown. Cut open hoagies and on one side spread ranch dressing, place slices of Colby jack cheese, slices of tomato, and salt and pepper to taste then sprinkle with parmesan cheese. On the other side of the hoagie place some slices of Mozzarella cheese, bacon, and cheddar cheese. Place buns on a cookie sheet and broil. Watch closely until the cheese bubbles and the edges of the hoagies are browning. Close the hoagie together and enjoy!
