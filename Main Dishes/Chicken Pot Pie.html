Chicken Pot Pie- Ann Romney

Chicken Mixture:

4 cups chicken broth
1 chicken bouillon cube
12 TBS butter
2 onions-minced
2 large carrots- diced
Salt and pepper to taste
½ cup flour
¼ cup heavy cream
1 pkg. (9 oz.) frozen peas
6 chicken breasts- cooked and diced (or leftover chicken or canned chicken)

Biscuits Supreme:

2 cups flour
4 tsp. baking powder
2 tsp. sugar
½ tsp. salt
½ cup butter
1 cup buttermilk

To make chicken mixture: Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onions and carrots. Sauté until tender. Season with salt and pepper. Add flour and cook, stirring constantly, for 2-3 minutes until blended. Stir in hot chicken broth. Add cream, peas, and chicken. Stir. Pour into 9X13 pan and bake at 375* for 20 minutes.

To make biscuits: Mix together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt just until blended. Roll out on a floured surface to 1 ½ inch thickness and cut into 3” rounds.

Place biscuits on top of chicken mixture and bake 12-15 more minutes or until the biscuits are golden brown. (If you bake the biscuits individually, place on a greased cookie sheet and bake at 450* for 10-12 minutes.)
