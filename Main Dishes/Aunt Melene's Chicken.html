Aunt Melene’s Chicken Marinade

¼ cup rice vinegar
½ tsp ginger
½ -3/4 cup brown sugar
½ cup soy sauce
½ cup water

Mix all the ingredients together and pour over chicken. Marinade in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes. Grill over low heat (the brown sugar will burn the outside before the inside can be cooked if it is on high heat.) Baste the chicken while cooking with the marinade. You can place the chicken in a pan of warm marinade after they are gilled to keep them moist and hot until serving time. Serves 6
