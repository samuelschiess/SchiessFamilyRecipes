Buttermilk Fried Chicken

Marinade:

2 lbs. chicken tenderloins or drumsticks (Note: boneless chicken cooks quickly. Chicken with bones will need to be baked for 45 minutes after browning.)
1 cup buttermilk
1 ½ tsp. salt
¼ tsp. cayenne pepper
¼ tsp. garlic powder
¼ tsp. paprika

Breading:

1 ½ cups flour
1 tsp. salt
¾ tsp. pepper
¾ tsp. garlic powder
¾ tsp. paprika
1 ½ tsp. baking powder
3 TBS buttermilk

Cooking:

3-4 cups vegetable oil

Mix the marinade. Add the chicken and marinade in the refrigerator overnight.

Mix breading until clumpy. Pour oil in a deep pot 3/4 “high. Bread the chicken.

Cook chicken on high. Don’t crowd the chicken. Cook 1 ½ minutes on each side- or until golden brown and cooked all the way through.

If the chicken has bones, brown it in oil and then bake it at 350* for 40 minutes.
