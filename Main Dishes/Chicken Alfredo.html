Chicken Alfredo (Olive Garden Copy Cat)

12 oz. fettuccine pasta- cooked until tender and drained
2 TBS olive oil
½ cup plus 2 TBS butter
1 pound chicken tenders
1 ½ tsp salt, divided
1 ½ tsp pepper, divided
3 cloves garlic, finely minced
1 ½ TBS flour
2 cups heavy cream
¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Place olive oil in a frying pan over high heat. Add 2 TBS butter. Quickly add chicken and season with 1 tsp each of salt and pepper. Cook until golden brown on one side, then flip the chicken over, reduce heat to medium, and cover the pan cooking the other side until golden brown (about 5 minutes). Make sure that the chicken is no longer pink on the center. Place on a plate and when they are cooled slice into chunks.

In a large, deep-sided pan, melt ½ cup butter over medium heat. Add garlic to the pan and cook until aromatic (about 30 seconds). Reduce heat to medium-low. Add remaining salt and pepper. Whisk flour into butter and stir until smooth. Slowly whisk in cream. Cook until sauce is steaming and slightly thickened. Add ½ cup parmesan and stir until smooth. Add chicken and pasta. Sprinkle with remaining parmesan. Serve!
