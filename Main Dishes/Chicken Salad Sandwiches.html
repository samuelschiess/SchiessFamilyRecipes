Chicken Salad Sandwiches

4 cups chicken- cubed
1 cup mayonnaise
1 tsp. Dijon mustard
½ tsp. dill
2 TBS parsley
2 tsp. lemon juice
2 tsp. salt
Pepper- to taste
1 stalk celery
Grapes-sliced in half
¼ cup onion

Mix all the ingredients together and serve on a croissant roll.
