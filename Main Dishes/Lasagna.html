Lasagna

1 lb. Jimmy Dean’s HOT sausage- cooked
1 can (12 oz.) spaghetti sauce
9 lasagna noodles- cooked
3 cups cheddar cheese- grated
1 carton (16 oz.) cottage cheese

Layer the ingredients in a 9x13 pan. ½ cup spaghetti sauce, 3 noodles, cottage cheese, 1 cup cheddar cheese, spaghetti sauce, sausage, noodles. Top with cheddar cheese. Bake at 350* for 20 minutes or until bubbly.
