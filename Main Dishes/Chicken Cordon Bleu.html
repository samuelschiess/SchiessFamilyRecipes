Chicken Cordon Bleu- Melinda Draper

12 boneless skinless chicken breasts
12 slices ham- cut thin
12 slices Swiss cheese
1 can cream of chicken soup
16 oz. sour cream
Butter- melted
Saltine crackers- crushed

Put ham and cheese on top of chicken and roll- hold together with a toothpick. Dip in melted butter and then in crushed saltine crackers. Bake at 350* for 1 hour- covered. Mix cream of chicken and sour cream together and pour over chicken and bake for 30 more minutes.
