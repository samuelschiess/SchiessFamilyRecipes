Meat Loaf-Great-Grandma Thomas

1 pound hamburger
1½ slices of bread, torn in pieces
1 egg, beaten
2 TBS diced onion
½ tsp salt
1/8 tsp each pepper, dry mustard, celery salt, and garlic salt
1½ tsp Worcestershire sauce
Ketchup

Mix all ingredients thoroughly. Put into a bread pan. Spread with ketchup. Bake at 350* for 1 hour.
