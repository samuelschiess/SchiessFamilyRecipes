Chicken Divine- Grandma Tippets

1 head of broccoli- cut up
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 cup mayonnaise (not miracle whip)
1½ cup rice- cooked
1 cup milk
1 can chicken

Place broccoli in a 9X13” pan. Mix milk, soup, and mayo together until smooth. Add chicken and cooked rice. Pour over broccoli. Top with cheese. Bake at 350* for 30 minutes.
