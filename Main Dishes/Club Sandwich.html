Club Sandwich

3 slices bread
3 slices turkey
2 slices bacon- cooked
2 slices tomato
1 leaf iceberg lettuce
Mayonnaise

Toast the bread. Arrange the sandwich from the bottom up as follows: toast, mayo, turkey, toast, mayo, bacon, tomato, lettuce, mayo, and toast. Place 4 toothpicks in the top of the sandwich, one at each corner, and slice into 4 sections. Makes 1 sandwich.
