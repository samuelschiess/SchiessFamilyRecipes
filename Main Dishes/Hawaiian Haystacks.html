Hawaiian Haystacks

4 cups rice- cooked
2 boneless chicken breasts (canned chicken or leftovers)
1 cube chicken bouillon
1 cup water
Â¼ tsp paprika
1 can cream of chicken soup
2 cups milk

Choice of toppings: Chow Mein noodles, tomatoes, olives, pineapple, peas, sunflower seeds, cheese, whatever sounds good.

Place chicken, bouillon, and water in saucepan. Sprinkle with paprika. Cover and cook until chicken is tender. Remove chicken and chop (You can also substitute leftover or canned chicken.)

Stir soup and milk together and heat until hot. To serve, place rice on a plate, top with sauce, and then add toppings.
