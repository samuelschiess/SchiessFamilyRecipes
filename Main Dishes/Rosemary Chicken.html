Rosemary Chicken

For the Chicken:

¼ cup flour
½ tsp. salt
Pepper to taste
14 oz. boneless, skinless chicken breasts- flattened to ¼” thick or chicken tenders
1 TBS olive oil

For Butter Sauce:

¾ cup chicken broth
2 TBS butter
1 TBS sage
1 TBS parsley
1 TBS rosemary

Dredge chicken in seasoned flour (flour, salt and pepper mixed together.) Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat and cook the chicken 2-3 minutes. (Don’t over crown the pan.) Turn the chicken over, cook 2-3 more minutes, until golden on the outside, no longer pink in the middle, and will easily cut with a fork. Transfer the chicken to a plate and keep warm. In the same skillet as before, add chicken broth and boil 4 minutes or until reduced by half. Remove from heat and whisk in butter and herbs. Serve chicken with herbed butter sauce.
