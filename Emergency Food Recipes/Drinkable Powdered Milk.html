Drinkable Powdered Milk


3 cups powdered milk
1-gallon water
1 ½ tsp sugar
½ tsp vanilla
Place ½ of the water in a 1-gallon jug. Add powdered milk, put lid on the jug and shake to mix. Add vanilla and sugar. Slowly add rest of water and mix again. Milk will be frothy so you won’t be able to add all the water now. Just remember to add a bit more later.
