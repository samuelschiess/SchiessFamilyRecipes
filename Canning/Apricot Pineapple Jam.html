Apricot Jam

6 cans crushed pineapple
8 cups sugar
Apricots- as many as you can fit into a large soup pot.

Mash and cook the apricots in the large soup pot over medium heat until boils and thickens. Add the sugar and cook until sugar dissolves. Pour into clean, hot bottles. (you may need to use a funnel for this part.) Place on sterilized lids and rings tightly.
