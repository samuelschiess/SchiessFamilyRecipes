Apple Sauce

4 apples-cored, peeled, and sliced (Gala apples are the best)
¼ cup sugar
1 tsp. cinnamon (if you desire)
Water- to help the blender cut up the apples
Fresh Fruit in a bowl of water- mix according to the directions on the package.

For a Large Quantity: This recipe is for a single batch of applesauce. When you have a lot of apples, add as many boiled apples as fit into your blender and add ¼ cup of sugar and 1 tsp. cinnamon to each blender full of applesauce.

Peel, core, and slice apples and place into the bowl of water with Fruit Fresh powder added (so the apples don’t go brown). When ready, place apples in a pot of boiling water until they are soft (10 minutes or less). Drain the apples well. Place into a blender, add sugar and cinnamon. Blend- adding just enough water to help it blend. Pour into HOT BOTTLES using a funnel (if the bottles are not hot, they will break when you put them into the canner!) Place bottles into the canner and process for 20 minutes.
