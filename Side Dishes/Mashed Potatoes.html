Mashed Potatoes

5-7 medium potatoes- peeled and cubed
Water- fill the pot up halfway
2 TBS. butter
¼ - ½ cup milk

Heat a pot of water over medium heat. Place in the potatoes and boil until tender (20 minutes or so). Drain the water. Add butter and milk. Mash with a potato masher until smooth. This is a recipe that is very forgiving. You can change the number of potatoes or make the potatoes creamier by adding more milk, or more butter.
