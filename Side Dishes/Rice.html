How to Cook Rice

Put desired amount of rice in a pot. (Our family usually uses 1 cup dried rice- it will double.)

Rinse with water and drain a few times until the water is clear.

Fill pot with water. Place your finger in the water and touch to top layer of rice. The water should come up to the first knuckle of your finger.

Place the pot on the stovetop on high heat.

Cook until boiling, and boil for 5 minutes.

Reduce heat to low and place lid on the pot.

Continue to cook for 15 minutes.

Take it off the heat and remove the lid.

It will be perfectly done!
