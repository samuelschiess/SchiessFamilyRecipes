Funeral Potatoes

2 lbs. frozen hash browns (or 8-10 potatoes- peeled, cubed, and microwaved until soft)
½ cup melted butter
1 tsp salt
½ tsp pepper
1 can Cream of Chicken Soup
1-pint sour cream
2 cups grated cheese
2 TBS minced onion

Toppings:

2 cups crushed corn flakes (can put in a bag and use a rolling pin to crush).
¼ cup melted butter

Thaw potatoes before mixing. (They cook and mix better.) Combine the first 8 ingredients and place in a 9x13 buttered pan. Sprinkle with crushed cornflakes, then drizzle with ¼ cup melted butter. Bake 1 hour at 325*.
