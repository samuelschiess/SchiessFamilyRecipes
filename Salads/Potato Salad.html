Potato Salad

1 cup mayonnaise
2 TBS. sweet pickle relish
1 small onion- chopped
2 eggs- hard boiled, peeled, and diced
1⁄2 tsp. salt (or more to taste)
1 1⁄2 TBS prepared mustard
1⁄2 tsp. sugar
4 cups potatoes- cooked and cubed

Mix all the ingredients together. Chill before serving.
