Broccoli/Bacon/Raisin Salad

1 bunch broccoli- washed, drained, broken into flowerets
1⁄2 cup red onion- chopped
1 cup celery- chopped
1 lb. bacon- fried crisp, drained, and crumbled
1⁄2 cup sunflower seeds- hulled and salted
1⁄2 cup raisins

Dressing:

3⁄4 cup mayonnaise
1⁄4 cup sugar
2 TBS. vinegar

Combine salad ingredients together in a large mixing bowl. Set aside. Combine dressing ingredients together mix thoroughly. Pour dressing over salad ingredients; stir to blend. Serve chilled. Refrigerate leftovers.
