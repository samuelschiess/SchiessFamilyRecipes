Corn Bread- Marie Calendar’s

1 1⁄4 cup flour
3⁄4 cup cornmeal
2 tsp baking powder 1/3 cup sugar
3⁄4 tsp salt
1 1⁄4 cup milk
1⁄4 cup shortening
1 egg

Mix dry ingredients in a bowl. In a separate bowl, mix the wet ingredients. Combine them. Pour into a greased 8x8” pan. Bake at 400* for 25-30 minutes.

Honey Butter

1 stick butter- softened 1/3 cup honey

Use mixer on high speed to whip until smooth and fluffy.
