Emily’s Muffins

1⁄2 cup sugar
1⁄2 cup butter- softened
1 egg
2 cups flour
1⁄2 tsp cinnamon
1⁄2 tsp salt
2 tsp baking powder
1 cup milk
1⁄4 cup brown sugar-for topping 1⁄2 tsp cinnamon- for topping

Cream together sugar, butter, and eggs. Add flour, cinnamon, salt baking powder, and milk. Mix well. In another bowl, combine brown sugar and cinnamon and sprinkle a little on the bottom of the paper muffin cup in the muffin tin. Pour dough mixture in the muffin cup and sprinkle a little of the brown sugar and cinnamon mixture on the top. Bake at 350* for 12-15 minutes. Makes 1 1⁄2 dozen.
