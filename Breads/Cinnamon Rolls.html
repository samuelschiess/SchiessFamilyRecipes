Cinnamon Rolls- Chiska Couture

4 1⁄2 tsp yeast- 2 pkgs. 1⁄2 cup warm water
1 1⁄2 cup milk
1⁄2 cup shortening
1⁄2 cups sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp salt
5 to 7 1⁄2 cups flour- you want them a little on the sticky side.

Dissolve yeast in water. Heat milk to lukewarm. Add to milk, shortening, sugar, eggs, and salt. Mix. Add yeast. Add flour. Knead 250 times. Place in cloth covered bowl and let rise until doubled. On a floured surface, roll out dough in a large rectangle/oval shape. Pour melted butter and toppings. Lift up long side of dough and roll up into cinnamon roll shape. Cut into 2” sections with a string. Place onto a large greased cookie sheet. Let rise again. Bake at 375* until browned. (22-25 minutes)

Orange Rolls:

1⁄2 cup sugar
1⁄2 cup butter-melted Zest from one orange

Melt butter and spread on the rolled-out dough. Sprinkle sugar and zest on top. Roll up rolls. Bake and frost with cream cheese frosting.

Lemon Rolls:

1⁄2 cup sugar
1⁄2 cup butter- melted Zest from 1-2 lemons 1⁄4 tsp nutmeg
1⁄2 tsp ground ginger

Melt butter and spread on the rolled-out dough. Sprinkle sugar, zest, and spices on top. Roll up rolls. Bake and frost with lemon glaze. (Juice of one lemon, 4 oz. cream cheese, 1 cup powdered sugar, lemon zest.)

Cream Cheese Frosting:

8 oz. cream cheese- softened 1⁄2 cup butter- softened
1 tsp vanilla
1 lb. powdered sugar

Beat together with a mixer. Thin out if needed- 1 TBS milk at a time.
