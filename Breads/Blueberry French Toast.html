Blueberry French Toast- Ann Romney (Holiday Breakfasts)

12 slices Texas toast or French Bread- cubed
1 pkg cream cheese- 8 oz.- cubed
1 cup fresh blueberries or 16 oz. frozen blueberries 12 eggs
2 cups milk
1⁄2 cup maple syrup

Spread 1⁄2 breadcrumbs on greased 9x13” pan. Scatter cream cheese cubes over bread. Cover with blueberries. Add remaining bread cubes. Combine eggs, milk, and syrup. Mix well. Pour over bread mixture. Cover with foil and refrigerate overnight. Bake at 350* covered for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake 30 more minutes or until set.
