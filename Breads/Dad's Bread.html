Dad’s Bread- Grandma Schiess

Single- 1 loaf
2 cups hot water 1⁄4 cup oil
1⁄4 cup sugar
1 TBS salt
2 cups flour
1 TBS yeast
2-3 cups more flour

Small Batch- 4 loaves

3 cups hot water 1⁄2 cup oil
1⁄2 cup sugar
2 TBS salt
3 cups flour
2 TBS yeast
4-6 cups more flour

Large Batch- 8 loaves (5 small and 3 big loaves)

5 cups hot water 2/3 cup oil
2/3 cup sugar
3 TBS salt
5 cups flour
3 TBS yeast
7-9 cups more flour

In a bowl, mix the first 6 ingredients. Mix well. Start kneading and add more flour until it forms a dough and is not sticky. Form into loaves and place into greased bread pans. Let rise until doubled. Bake at 325* for 35 minutes on the “Convection Bake” setting.
