Berry Cheesecake Muffins

1/3 cup butter-softened 3⁄4 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 1⁄2 cup flour
1 1⁄2 tsp baking powder 1 tsp cinnamon
1/3 cup milk

Cream Cheese Filling:

6 oz. cream cheese- softened 1/3 cup sugar
1 egg
3⁄4 cup fresh raspberries
3⁄4 cup fresh blueberries

Streusel Topping:

1⁄4 cup flour
2 TBS brown sugar 1⁄2 tsp cinnamon
1 TBS butter- cold

In a larger mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add eggs, beat well. Combine the flour, baking powder, and cinnamon: add to creamed mixture alternately with milk. Fill greased or paper-lined muffin cups half full.

For filling, in a small mixing bowl, beat cream cheese, sugar, and egg until smooth. Fold in the berries. Drop a rounded TBS into the center of each muffin. For topping, combine the flour, brown sugar, and cinnamon in a small bowl: cut in butter until crumbly. Sprinkle over batter.

Bake at 375* for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
