Zucchini Bread/Banana Bread- Karalee

3 cups whole wheat flour 1 TBS cinnamon
1 1⁄2 tsp baking soda
1⁄4 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
2 eggs
3⁄4 cup oil
1 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla
3 cups zucchini- grated (or for Banana Bread 1-3 ripe bananas- mashed)
1 cup chocolate chips
1 cup nuts- chopped (optional)

In a large bowl, mix dry ingredients, then add eggs, oil, and vanilla. Mix. Then gently mix in zucchini (or bananas) and chocolate chips. Bake at 325*. For 1 large loaf- 50-60 minutes. For 12 muffins- 18-22 minutes. This recipe doubles beautifully.
