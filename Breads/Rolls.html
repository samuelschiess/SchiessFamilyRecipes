Rolls- Danica Foltz

Mix:

2 cups warm water- warm enough that you would like to shower in it, but not too hot.
1 TBS salt
1⁄2 cup sugar
1/3 cup oil
2 cups flour
2 eggs

(You should have a soupy mess.)

Add:

1 TBS yeast
4-5 cups more flour

When the dough is too stiff to stir, start kneading in the flour. When the dough is no longer sticky, you have enough flour. Knead the dough 8 minutes, or until elastic. Pour some vegetable oil into a small bowl. Cover your hands in oil and shape the dough into golf ball sized rolls. Place rolls on greased cookie sheet about 1” apart. Rub some vegetable oil on top of each roll so they stay moist while raising. Cover with plastic wrap and let rise 3-4 hours (or until doubled in size). Bake at 350* for 15-20 minutes. Rub butter on top of each roll while still hot. Makes about 30 rolls.
