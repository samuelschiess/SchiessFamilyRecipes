Breadsticks- Felix (Kristin Pickering)

2 TBS sugar
1 TBS yeast
1 1⁄2 cup water (lukewarm) 1 1⁄2 tsp salt
3 + cups flour

Mix sugar and yeast. Add water. Mix and let sit 2-3 minutes until doubled. Add salt and flour. Mix until the dough is too stiff then knead 8 minutes. Let sit 10-20 minutes. Roll out on a cookie sheet. Cover with melted butter, parmesan cheese, garlic salt, or whatever sounds good. Let rise 15-20 minutes more. Score bread stick lines with a knife. Bake at 375* for 15-20 minutes or until brown on top.
