McCormick’s Honey Glazed Ham

1 bone-in spiral-cut ham (10 lbs.)
1 cup honey
1⁄2 cup brown sugar- firmly packed
1 tsp. ground ginger
1 tsp. ground mustard
1⁄2 tsp cinnamon

Pre-heat oven to 325*. Place ham on its side in a 2” deep roasting pan. Mix honey, brown sugar, and spices until blended. If too thick, microwave on high for 30 seconds to 1 minute, stirring after each 30 seconds. Brush 1⁄2 mixture over the ham, gently separating slices so that the mixture can reach the middle of the ham. Cover loosely with tin foil. Bake ham 1 hour, basting occasionally with drippings. Remove foil. Brush with remaining honey mixture. Bake 45 minutes longer.
