Buttermilk Syrup:

1⁄2 cup butter- 1 stick
1⁄2 cup buttermilk
1 cup sugar
1 tsp corn syrup
1 tsp soda 1 tsp vanilla

Combine buttermilk, butter, sugar, and corn syrup in a saucepan. Bring to a boil. Stir until butter is melted and sugar is dissolved. Remove from heat and add soda and vanilla. Serve warm.
