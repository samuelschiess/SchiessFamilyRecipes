Pizza Hut Pizza Sauce

1 can (8oz.) tomato sauce
1 tsp dry oregano
1⁄2 tsp dry basil
1⁄2 tsp garlic salt

Combine all ingredients together. Spoon on top of pizza crust (don’t use too much) and top with a lot of mozzarella cheese and desired toppings.
