Chicken Noodle Soup – Levi’s version

6 cups water
2 chicken bouillon cubes
1 cup chicken- leftovers or canned
5 potatoes- cubed
3 carrots- sliced
1 onion- chopped
8 oz. extra wide egg noodles
1 tsp. garlic salt
1 tsp. salt
¼ tsp. pepper

In a pot over medium heat add water, bouillon cubes, chicken, potatoes, carrots, and onions. Bring to a boil and cook until the potatoes and carrots are tender. Add egg noodles and boil for 7 more minutes until tender. Add the seasonings and serve.
