Aunt Karen’s Soup (Vegetable Corn Chowder with Chicken)

Cook 2 chicken breasts in 6 cups of water with 4 TBS chicken bouillon and 1 tsp. salt until done. Then set aside to cool before cutting into small pieces. (You can also use leftover chicken or canned chicken.)

Add to chicken broth: (or if you use leftover or canned chicken, pour 6 cups of water into a pot and 2 chicken bouillon cubes and then add these next ingredients.)

1 large stalk celery- chopped
1 small onion- chopped
1 cup rice
1 grated carrot
3 cloves garlic- minced
3 cups potatoes- cubed into about ½ inch pieces
Cook until rice and potatoes are tender, about 15 to 20 minutes.

Add the chicken and:

1 TBS sugar
1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese (or 3 TBS dried)
2 cans chicken broth
2 cans corn (or frozen corn)
1 cup milk
2 cups half and half
½ tsp dried parsley

Mix 3 TBS flour with about ¾ cup of liquid and pour into chowder to thicken. Simmer on low an additional 20 minutes or so. Add salt and pepper to taste.
