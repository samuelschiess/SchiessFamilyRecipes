White Chili- Kristin Pickering

1 ½ cups dried navy beans
3 (10 oz. cans) chicken broth
1 cup water
1 onion- chopped
1 clove garlic- minced
¼ tsp salt
2 cups chopped cooked chicken
1 (4 oz.) can chopped green chilies
1 tsp ground cumin
3/4 tsp dried oregano
¼ tsp ground red pepper
1/8 tsp ground cloves
¾ cup Monterey Jack cheese

Sort and wash beans. Soak in water for 8 hours. Drain the beans and return to pan. Add broth and next 4 ingredients. Bring to a boil, cover, reduce heat and simmer for 2 hours, stirring occasionally.

Add chicken, chilies, cumin, oregano, red pepper and clove to pan, cover and cook for 30 minutes. Serve with grated cheese, sour cream, and corn chips.
