Chicken Noodle Soup- Grandma Tippets

Soup:

4 cups water
2 chicken bouillon cubes
1 onion (or 1 tsp onion powder)
1 tsp. garlic salt
Chicken (left overs or canned)

Mix all the ingredients in a pot and bring to a boil.

Noodles (Better Homes and Gardens Cookbook page 266):

1 cup flour
¼ tsp. salt
1 egg- beaten
1/6 cup water
½ tsp. olive oil

Mix flour and salt together. Make a well in the center of the mixture. Pour egg, water, and oil in it. Mix well and then knead 8 minutes. Cover and let rest for 10 minutes. Use a pasta machine to roll and cut pasta.

Add the noodles to the boiling soup. Let noodles cook for 7 minutes. Serve over mashed potatoes.
