Potato Soup- Grandma Tippets

1/3 cup butter
1/3 cup flour
3½ cup milk
4 large potatoes, boiled or you can bake these ahead of time
¼ cup diced onions
12 bacon strips, cooked and crumbled
1 ¼ cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup sour cream
1 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper

Boil potatoes. While they are boiling, melt butter in a pan. Stir in flour until smooth. Gradually add milk, stirring constantly until thick (this will take a while- be patient). Add boiled potatoes and onions. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Reduce heat. Simmer for 10 minutes. Add the remaining ingredients and stir until the cheese is melted. Serve.
