Stew

Leftover pot roast or pork roast
3 carrots- chopped
1 onion- chopped
5 potatoes- chopped
2 beef bouillon cubes
6 cups of water
1 beef (or pork or chicken) gravy mix packet
1 tsp salt
1 tsp garlic salt

Fill a pot with the water. Bring to a boil on medium heat. While it is heating, add carrots, onion, potatoes, bouillon cubes, and meat. Let it boil until the vegetables are tender. Pour the gravy mix into a separate bowl. Add some of the hot broth from the pot. Mix until smooth and add back into the pot so that it will thicken up. Add salt and garlic salt to taste.
